double StatsCalculator::computeMean ( )
private

Private method that actually computes the mean of the stored numeric values.

Returns
The computed mean is returned as a double-precision value.

The mean of a sequence of numbers is equal to their sum divided by their multiplicity.

Note
Computation of the required sum can be delegated to the computeSum() method of StatsCalculator, while the number of elements in the sequence is provided by the size() method of the std::vector<double>-type member datum "numericValues".
